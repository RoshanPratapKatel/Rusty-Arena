1. The World (The Grid)

    Space: A 2D Grid (Terminal window), approx 80 columns wide x 24 rows high.

    Coordinate System: (0,0) is Top-Left. y increases downwards. x increases rightwards.

    Boundaries: Objects cannot leave the box.

2. The Warrior (The Player)

    Location: Locked to the Bottom Row. Can only move Left (x-1) and Right (x+1).

    Health: Starts at 100 HP.

    Action: Can fire Bullets vertically upwards.

    Damage Rule: The Warrior takes 25 Damage if a Goblin successfully hits the ground. (The penalty is for failing to defend).

    Healing Rule: The Warrior regains health by catching (colliding with) falling Healing Packages.

3. The Goblins (The Enemy)
    
    The Horde: There is a total "Global Health Pool" of 1000 HP to defeat.

    Spawning: Individual Goblins spawn at the Top Row at random x positions.

    Stats: Each Goblin carries a chunk of the global HP (e.g., one might have 10 HP, another 50 HP).

    Movement: They fall Down (y+1) one step at a time.

    Death: A Goblin dies if its HP reaches 0 (from Bullets).

4. The Projectiles (Bullets)

    Origin: Spawn at Warrior's current x, just above the Warrior.

    Movement: Fly Up (y-1) one step at a time.

    Collision: If a Bullet hits a Goblin, the Goblin loses HP, and the Bullet disappears.

5. Game Loop

    Win Condition: The Global Horde Pool reaches 0 (All goblins spawned and destroyed).

    Lose Condition: Warrior HP reaches 0.
